# Chapter 232

Ho Yoowon.

The director of this company who'd know more about Jang Heoun than anyone else.

"You must have a lot you're curious about."

But he was an untrustworthy man, and he was smiling at me.

Here, in a corridor turned skeletal ruin of corpses by fire and smoke.

He asks me if I have anything to ask.

'...In that case.'

I can approach it this way.

I let the smoke rise.

*Proposition : One-on-one Q&A Ritual*

"Mm?"

*Conditions : 2 participants*

*► Questions and answers must alternate in strict order.*

*► One must always answer the other's question truthfully.*

*► If unable to answer, one must state aloud, "Unable to respond," then receive the next question.*

*► An answer cannot be delayed for more than 96 seconds.*

[Ah, fascinating. Setting such specific conditions for no reason. It has a slightly ritualistic, incantatory feel to it...]

I did it deliberately. Because this is with something not human.

And...

'This one definitely has questions for me too.'

In this formal, ritualized form of exchanging questions, he can't help but take the bait.

And.

*► If one deliberately conceals the truth during the ritual, punishment is inflicted.*

"...Haha! Are you imitating me?"

Yes. Have a taste of what it's like to be on the other side.

Ho Yoowon laughed a few times, then answered brightly.

"But I like it."

Yet, a faint gloom rippled in his eyes.

"Fair, isn't it? I could impose a binding to enforce punishment... but I won't. I want to trust you, Roe Deer-nim."

'He tried to bind me and got punched for it.'

But it doesn't matter.

*: One-on-one Q&A Ritual*
