# Chapter 227

Let’s think this through.

Baek Saheon left this paper boat behind in Jisan Village. Most likely, it was meant for the “Agent Grapes” he met there.

Of course, he wouldn’t know the agent’s codename was *Grapes*.

*'If he had known that, he would have already realized I’m Kim Soleum.'*

Since he didn’t know, he left behind a communication item.

So in this situation, it would be better to respond like an agent if I wanted the conversation to continue.

Because my melted body was stuffed into the suit, the distribution of force was uneven, and my handwriting trembled slightly.

@r@*continuously to toher supernatural disasters,*@r@

@r@*once I obtain one later. Thank you.*@r@

*'If I find and reassemble a digestive system someday, maybe I’ll actually be able to eat them.'*

A solo night shift.

*'If it were me, I wouldn’t try something like this….'*

But then again, that’s exactly why this place is a crazy company in a horror-story world.

So I was left alone at the front desk in the form of an open transport cage.

"......"

“Th-thank you…?”
