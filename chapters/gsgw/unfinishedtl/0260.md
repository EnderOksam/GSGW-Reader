# Chapter 259

It was the very next day that I met Section Chief Lee Jaheon after he escaped from the Groom Lesson ghost story.

It was a meet-up arranged with the agents who had obtained permission for a short outing to stabilize my condition (Condition : There must be no people nearby), and for a follow-up strategy meeting.

There was already an early guest in the pavilion of the old park that was our destination.

Ho Yoowon.

"Welcome, Roe Deer-nim!"

The director, who somehow knew we were going to talk about the Sekwang City exploration, had once again beaten us to it and taken the lead.

And there was one person sitting upright next to Ho Yoowon.

"Additional personnel was needed for the exploration project, correct? It seemed you were eager to meet Mr. Roe Deer, so we came along immediately."

A familiar lizard looked at me with vertical pupils.

"Mr. Roe Deer."

Section Chief Lee Jaheon.

*Greeting and well-being check*

"Yes. I have returned to work."

From the circumstances, it seems he came looking for me that very day I was rescued, but considering my situation, he chose the right timing that would let us meet smoothly.

'As expected of a senior employee of this era...'

He is a person of a different character from a certain director.

Section Chief Lee Jaheon did not show a flicker of agitation even when he saw my Security Team state, which would look quite bizarre.

He had already seen me like this when we entered the resort of the Cheerful Theme Park last time, and even then Chief Lizard recognized me and treated me exactly as before.

Just like now.

"Since your return, have any other anomalies occurred to you, Mr. Roe Deer?"

*No anomalies*

"I can confirm this."

And even Assistant Manager Eun Haje began talking as if nothing were unusual.

"Well now, seeing you after a while, your face is shining, Section Chief. Ah, it is a figure of speech. A figure of speech."

"? Yes."

By their standards, this was an exceptionally warm and friendly lizard-style conversation.

But to the agents, it seems this scene came across as bizarre...

Agent Choi barely managed to join the conversation.

"Ah, really. You are quite handsome, Mr. Civilian."

"Yes."

'Fuck!'

Baek Saheon was swearing violently on the inside.

No, it's that he could *only* swear on the inside.

Because he was gagged, covered with a cloth, and being transported somewhere!

*-- Supervisor Baek Saheon, Director Ho is requesting your presence.*

He had gone up to the director's office after that summons, and this insanity had happened.

'...Am I actually fucking screwed?'

This was the intuition of someone who had worked at a deranged ghost story company was screaming.

With cold sweat running down his skin, he desperately waited for a chance to bolt in case he was dragged off to the Research Team, and only a few minutes had passed.

The movements stopped, and the cloth covering his head was removed.

"...!!"

And what he saw before him was...

'W-What is this.'

The Fox Counseling Office.

More precisely, seated at the waiting room table of the Fox Counseling Office were people wearing masks.

Two of them he knew.

A lizard mask and a falcon mask.

'W-Wait.'

One was someone who had gone missing and returned just yesterday, and the other was someone who had already quit long ago.

At that point, a thought flickered through Baek Saheon's mind.

'Director Ho's project team?!'

It was the rumored existence of the 'project' known only to those in Director Ho's circle. Even Baek Saheon, as a supervisor, had heard of it.

'Are they trying to assign me to the project?'

But in the next moment.

Baek Saheon noticed how the two people sitting in the table's shadows were dressed.

'...Disaster Bureau!'

They were government agents. However...

The agents were wearing masks.

'What the heck.'

*-- As agents, we cannot reveal our identities to a human who is dangerously untrustworthy.*

*-- Then why don't you wear masks as well?*

*-- Oh.*

And so they'd hurriedly procured them.

The two people wearing the nobleman and butcher Hahoe masks looked extremely suspicious, yet in another sense they also gave off the impression of strong Supernatural Disaster Management Bureau agents.

In any case, the simple fact that Disaster Management Bureau agents were calmly sitting together with Daydream employees was shocking enough for Baek Saheon.

But then.

"Supervisor Goat."

"......Yes."

The person Baek Saheon was most conscious of at this table was Director Ho, seated there.

He sat smiling with the same ever-youthful face as always, but strangely, his eyes looked filled with exhilaration.

Baek Saheon recognized those eyes.

The eyes of a fanatic with his goal right in front of them.

"Today, we've gathered here to offer you a very good proposal, Supervisor."

"...A proposal, sir?"

"I run a small project, and I've been selecting people to join me."

As expected.

"Congratulations, Saheon-ssi. You'll be working in the project team from now on."

Suspicious.

'Introducing me like this instead of issuing an official appointment?'

It felt cult-like in some way.

He grew even more wary, but first he desperately forced a smiling face.

Because he had to lie.
