# Chapter 234

Let us serve Ireum-nim.

Let us serve Ireum-nim.

Let us serve Ireum-nim.

Let us serve Ireum-nim.

Let us serve Ireum-nim…

Even though my ears are buried in flesh, the words echo. The language of a fanatic from the Happy Ending Sect, a faction of the Church of the Luminous Unknown, reverberates through the space and shakes the elevator…

……

Suddenly.

The elevator I am riding in is now in a different place.

*Drip.*

A dark space where the sound of a water droplet falling echoes.

A basement.

An ominous atmosphere I cannot identify. Damp chill, gloominess, mold-smelling wood, and …

A corpse.

It is Kim Heoun’s body, split into two.

At some point they have been carefully arranged and displayed in the dark basement. Inside a coffin filled with dozens of dried chrysanthemums and sunflowers, along with children’s origami.

#*I became happy*#

Blood drips down the chrysanthemums.

*Do you want to become happy too?*

The corpse in the coffin draws closer. Fear, the screams of those who are forgotten and erased, resignation, the only escape, the final happiness a human can grant another human, a painless death, and the end of a story …

*Do you want to become at peace too?*

But …

Of course it is frightening. The end of a story—an instinctive fear of death. Ah, it would have been better not to know. If it had been a death met in ignorance, without knowing anything, how peaceful the heart might have been.

But you have already come to know. A death chosen by oneself loses the meaning of happiness.

And so, there is only one salvation left for you …
