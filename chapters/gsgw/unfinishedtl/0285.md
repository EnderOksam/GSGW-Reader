# Chapter 284

"With this many explorations piling up, I figured it was about time you discovered it……."

Ho Yuwon’s voice sounded strange.

It seemed filled with joy, yet also sorrow, dim fear, and trembling all at once. At the same time, it felt as though he were smoothly concealing all of it beneath the surface.

But one thing was certain.

*'About time I discovered it?'*

That meant the traces of the Azure Dragon Team I found in Segwang Special City were exactly what Ho Yuwon had been expecting.

*Question: What do you know about the Deployment and Rescue Division Azure Dragon Team?*

"Hm? Isn’t that something Roe Deer should be explaining to me? That’s the reason I planned this project in the first place. To uncover the truth behind a certain Catastrophe-Level supernatural disaster. So……."

Ho Yuwon grabbed my shoulders and stared into me.

"What did you discover in Segwang Special City?"

At that moment, I became certain.

"About the Azure Dragon Team."

This man had known about the Azure Dragon Team from the very beginning.

And it was deeply connected to Ho Yuwon’s purpose in exploring the 'sealed Segwang Special City'….

*'Resentment, maybe?'*

I couldn’t be certain, but in my mind the pieces naturally connected together.

The deep grudge Ho Yuwon held toward the Disaster Management Bureau was undoubtedly tied, in some way, to the vanished Azure Dragon Team.

After hesitating for a moment, I explained the truth exactly as it was.

*Discovery at Segwang Technical High School:*

*Confirmed traces of the Azure Dragon Team within the school.*

"Ah."

He stiffened.

"So you really could reach it through the subway station too……."

*'As expected.'*

The joy of someone whose prediction had been correct.

*Question: What do you know about Segwang Technical High School?*

"Hm? Daydream enters that high school every new moon, don’t they? After seeing the name, I figured it must’ve been connected to Segwang Special City."

*Judgment: False*

"......."

Ho Yuwon’s eyes darkened strangely.

He looked at me.

*Question: What did Ho Yuwon do in Segwang Special City before the lockdown?*

"……Are you curious?"

*Affirmative.*

"I used to do the same thing I do now."

His eyes shifted toward the plaque hanging in front of the waiting room.

#><Fox Counseling Room><#

"Counseling."

…….

"It was rewarding work. Because of that, there were certain high schools I regularly visited in order to counsel the students there."

…!

The Fox Counseling Room seemed to warp.

"I did many different things for my clients……."

And the next moment—

"But all of that is in the past now."

Everything returned to normal again.

Ho Yuwon smiled gently at me.

…….

*Question: What is Ho Yuwon searching for in Segwang Special City?*

"What I’m searching for."

Ho Yuwon’s voice lowered.

"It’s located in a facility connected to one of the subway stations in Segwang Special City."

…….

"Please continue exploring in the direction you’ve been heading now, Roe Deer. Explore the subway stations and infer what the outside situation was like. If you do that, I think you’ll eventually find it. And once you face it……."

A smile spread across his lips once more.

"At that point, even if I say nothing, you’ll immediately understand what Ho Yuwon has been searching for."

…….

*Question: Ho Yuwon’s feelings toward the Azure Dragon Team*

"Who knows. I suppose I dislike them? I hate the Disaster Management Bureau, after all."

But the voice giving that answer was strangely dry.

And I sank deeper into confusion.

The Azure Dragon Team.

Segwang Special City.

And… Ho Yuwon.

"……First, let’s move somewhere else."

The agents’ expressions were grim.

Under the cognitive distortion provided by the dokkaebi fire, I followed them once more into a nearby Disaster Management Bureau container.

I didn’t even have time to worry about things like, 'At this rate, won’t the Disaster Management Bureau discover where I am?'

"…Grapes. You see, we found something."

The agents’ faces darkened as they added that it was a bad sign.

"But we managed to piece together the general sequence of events. Listen……."

The Disaster Management Bureau’s response records leading up to the notation written on the paper: Catastrophe-Level Supernatural Disaster – Special City Deletion.

By erasing it, so that it would never be recognized.

Agent Bronze and Agent Choi.
