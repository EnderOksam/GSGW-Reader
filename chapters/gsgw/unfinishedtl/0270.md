# Chapter 269

Results of the second Sekwang Special Metropolitan City expedition.

"Rescue... successful."

News came in that Assistant Manager Lee Seonghae had regained consciousness at the Disaster Management Bureau.

And the gimmick of 'if you die in Sekwang City's subway, you wake up in reality' also worked successfully.

"Everyone was mostly fine, aside from the fact that their bullet-shot heads hurt like hell. That Highborn Civil Servant even woke up smiling."

"They were smiling when they woke up, but once they understood the situation, the smiles disappeared."

Except for me.

After that, for a little over an hour, I remained unconscious.

"If you'd been any later, we were seriously about to make actual plans to head straight into the city. Thankfully you woke up before that..."

...Huu.

And then I saw the interior scenery, warm and soft, as if bright sunlight that didn't suit me at all were pouring in.

The Fox Counseling Office.

As promised, Ho Yoowon had 'intercepted' the passage transmitted to the Disaster Management Bureau through the well and connected it somewhere else.
