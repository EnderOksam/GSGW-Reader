# Chapter 243

An agent of the Disaster Management Bureau.

And the being his jakdu was pointing at.

Ho Yoowon.

A supernatural disaster.

"......"

"As soon as we're back at the bureau, I'm filing the report. 'Black-Tortoise Team 1 seeks formal approval to register the entity in question as a supernatural disaster with the aim of termination.'"

Agent Choi and Ho Yoowon locked eyes.

Between cold, set faces, the agent's gaze burned.

"Let's see if that insane cult company that grinds people up will keep bailing out an executive once he's been tagged by the bureau."

"...Ah."

And.

"What, you'll register me as a supernatural disaster and have the bureau manage me directly? Until I die? Until I'm terminated?"

The profane presence beyond the black smoke.

"That's awfully cruel."

It spoke in a smooth voice.

"A government agent threatening a civilian... It's not like I killed anyone right in front of you."

But Ho Yoowon's eyes were flat.

"Someone had to die anyway, didn't they? If the bureau does the choosing, does that make death not death? Does that make it rational?"

"......"

"While you're in the business of selecting the 'more acceptable' dead... did you think something like me wouldn't emerge?"

Ryu Jaekwan ground his teeth.

Then.

"Eh. So does that mean you'll just kill whoeverr?"

"......!"

"We were just talking about how, if someone has to die, we won't kill any good people. You're the one who, without any agreement, went and shoved a person, Directorr."

Brushing dust from herself as she walked up was Assistant Manager Lee Seonghae.

Ho Yoowon's smile deepened.

"It sounds like you're saying non-cooperation with the group is bad... That worries me a bit. You belong to the company. Are you allowed to defy your superior and make personal accusations?"

"Whyy? I didn't attack you. And that's a weird read. I'm just saying you did something bad."

Lee Seonghae glanced at Director Ho as if baffled, then hopped over to stand by the employee.

"Also, I'm on leave so I'm not on the clock. It's finee!"

"Wow. How brave."

"Yup. Kinda my thing!"

"......"

"......"

"...Well, not that it matters."

After weighing the scales, Ho Yoowon finally turned his head, choosing to ignore the annoying employee.

Toward 130666.

"Mr. Employee. Will you continue to accompany me for my project? As part of your duty."

And, appended.

"And those folks keep interfering with your work... I'd like you to restrain them a bit. For security."

"...!"

The agents' expressions hardened.

The yellow lantern-light under the black shadow swept over the agents...

Then looked back at the director.

*Denied*

"...!"

*Reason :*

*► Existing assignment (Church of the Luminous Unknown exploration) concluded*

*► Damaged uniform*

*Shall return to Isolation Room and report to the supervising researcher*

Uniform?

At that moment, they noticed it.

From inside the employee's coat, through torn fabric, something was seeping out.

Something amorphous.

Clotted, flesh-like masses, overflowing as if losing their form and forcing their way out.

130666 pressed the spot with a gloved hand and covered it with black mist, very much as if conscious of being watched.

Someone even caught a faint signal in between.

A trace of something like shame.

"......!"

Agent Choi's eyes changed.

Hope.

"We can fix that uniform too."

The employee's gaze met his.

"Come with us. We'll set you up real nice, even run a line for you. The workshop folks have serious skills."

But.

*Impossible*

*Reason : Proposal violates work rules*

"...Work rules? Can you explain what that means in more detail?"

*Impossible*

*Reason : Proposal violates work rules*

"......"

"Hang on. Are you not considering switching jobs at all?"

The gaze dipped to Lee Seonghae.

*Resignation impossible*

*Reason : Proposal violates work rules*

"Not even resignationn? Eh, that's..."

And before Lee Seonghae could finish, the employee turned his body.

He was about to leave immediately and head back.

Down to Daydream's underground.

To the Isolation Room.

"...Wait!"

Agent Choi hurried forward and blocked the way.

"I'll help you return."

......

*Question : Reason*

"You pulled me out of the box," Ryu Jaekwan spoke up.

"...! Right. We owe you."

Accepting his help without hesitation, Agent Choi continued.

"We're near Cheonan. Even if you quick-step it all the way to the Seoul HQ, it's still hours on foot, yeah?"

Instead of his usual rebuttal, Ryu Jaekwan quietly stepped to his side.

"You don't have a car, and you can't teleport. Hm? We'll take you."

......

Silence.

And then.

*Question : Means of transport*

Gotcha.

"Were you worried we'd stick you on a bicycle? Nah. We'll use something else."

Agent Choi started walking, casually guiding him.

To the well.

"It's a secret, but this well's on a good spot. Ley lines run here, and back in the day the bureau used it as a corridor for emergency deployments to the provinces. They say it got sealed due to procedural issues."

An added note, because of that numinous function's symbolism, when this village became a supernatural disaster site, they'd also used it as an escape point.

"Remember? When you came for the village festival, you took the manual with the tips I wrote. It mentioned the well. Haha. How was it, reading that?"

After another beat of silence.

*Unable to confirm*

......

"That happens. Don't worry."

And...

"Anyway, we don't use the well now... but if we send the agreed signal, the transit function should come back online, so let's just go ahead and try it."

Of course...

This was a lie.

'Records exist, but there's no way it would come back.'

The Disaster Management Bureau isn't stupid. If it were a corridor that could be reactivated, they'd have moved it.

But it sounded plausible enough.

"I'm going to use that to move us. Close your eyes and when you open them, we'll be in Seoul. Sound good?"

That won't happen.

The plan was simply to put him down into the well, herd him into a space as enclosed as possible so he couldn't run, and then use the transport ability.

'A makeshift Glass Prison.'

...It was a method he'd rather not use if he could help it.

There was no other way.

"Using this well won't get your identity handed over to the bureau or anything like that. I swear on the dokkaebi."

He could swear any which way he liked.

They weren't going to use the well anyway.

'...Did he catch on?'

It was possible. This damn hoobae had a filthy knack for catching on, and only at moments like this...

Really, he was born to be an agent.

'Please.'

The way that director Ho just stood there also bothered him. He'd expected him to try something, to interfere, or pull some trick to take the company employee back. But strangely, he kept quiet and behaved.

And Assistant Manager Lee Seonghae following along didn't feel great either.

But he couldn't tell them to quit it. He couldn't risk tipping anyone off that something was off.

Agent Choi moved with his nerves wound taut.

Behind him, Ryu Jaekwan would be getting his own makeshift Glass Prison ready.

'Thanks, Jaekwan-ah.'

Some part of his heart broke seeing his hoobae---who had no talent for lying, and who had just now made it out alive---was moving fast to back him up however he could.

Agent Choi kept walking.

*Step.*

*Step.*

With the sound of footsteps, the well drew near...

"Here."

Ryu Jaekwan quickly pried off the blocked top of the well.

...The trapped, damp air exhaled out and left a strange smell.

Drawing a breath, Agent Choi turned to 130666... No, to Kim Soleum, with a smile.

"...It'd be hard for you to go first, right? I'll go in and show you it's safe. See? There might be other things down there."

The gas mask stared at him in silence.

Agent Choi flashed a grin and set a foot into the well.

'The footholds... luckily, they're still there.'

They hadn't caved in.

Carefully wedging his feet against the well wall, he climbed down as fast as he could.

To lure the other in with no variables.

And when his feet finally touched the bottom...

"Oh! Looks like this'll work! Come on down."

At his shout, the horned one peered inside.

"...As soon as you're down, I'll try sending the transport signal."

Please.

......

Tap.

Black work boots walked down the well wall, descending.

The smoke drifted down and pooled at the floor, but true to a supernatural phenomenon, 130666's body arrived inside the well in a way that ignored physical norms.

'Ha...'

A surge rose up Choi's throat. Nausea or something else, he couldn't tell. But right now, there was no time to dwell on it.

'I have to do this.'

To make the act more convincing, he reached through the black smoke and set the dokkaebi lantern on the exact center of the well floor.

As if he were really about to send the transport signal.

In truth, it was a different, tacit signal.

One for Ryu Jaekwan.

"Alright, then..."

Jaekwan had to, right now, help him capture this thing.

But, at that moment.

Light burst out of the dokkaebi lantern on the floor.

"...!!"

Wait.

'This is...'

Something you only saw when a transport signal actually activated at a true ley-line node.

Was this really going to transport them?

'To where?'

And the strange things didn't stop there.

Amid the black smoke, something akin to the lantern's light gleamed from 130666's body.

A small rectangle glowed inside an inner pocket.

"...?"

As if to check it himself, 130666 took it out.

It was... something in the form of an employee badge. But not Daydream's.

The company name printed there was...

'Cheerful Research Institute...?'

In that instant.

The light grew stronger.

"...!"

Those watching from above realized entirely what was happening.

'Damn!'

Ryu Jaekwan hesitated for a beat, then gritted his teeth and hurled himself down into the well. He couldn't let just those two be transported.

And peering in after them, Lee Seonghae did the same.

"Oh."

Sensing the situation was unusual, and in order to 'help' good people, she jumped into the well.

She had taken a vacation day today, so she had plenty of time!

Thus, a moment later.

When Ho Yoowon quietly approached the well and looked down, he saw this.

"Ah."

There was nothing in the well.

Hands tingling with rapture, Ho Yoowon gripped the stones at the well mouth tight.
