The Cheerful Research Institute office.

Director Ho.

A black roulette.

Something dangerous.

...Hell?

A strange choice of words.

But what was more important...

And that name.

Do not call me by my real name.

"But Soleum-nim is still Soleum-nim, right?"

Was it deliberate?

Hearing my real name spoken in the middle of a Darkness made every nerve stand on end.

Especially here, in the Cheerful Research Institute.

And even more so because it was coming from Director Ho.

"If it bothers you so much, I can change how I address you. How about Roe Deer-nim?"

I stared at him.

"Silence means yes, then, Roe Deer-nim!"

"I know you're someone very special. Special enough that the last remaining researchers of the Cheerful Research Institute risked everything to summon you and smuggle you out, and..."

Director Ho's voice flattened, stripped of all expression.

"Special enough that, despite never running any intelligence operations, you could tell me information about Sekwang Special Metropolitan City as if you already knew it when I asked."

......

'So he was suspecting me.'

When I handed over intel and claimed my Wish Ticket, I hadn't cared if he found me suspicious.

I never imagined I'd run into him again like this.

But I realized something.

'He doesn't know who I really am.'

That I'd come from a world without ghost stories, where this world existed only as the <Dark Exploration Records> wiki.

Even if he'd seen the experiment logs I had read while rummaging through this underground Cheerful Research Institute, he didn't have the full picture.

Which meant...

He wanted information from me.

And I had no reason---nor intention---to give him any answers.

I resumed my search.

"How about a trade? We exchange what we know. About who we really are."
