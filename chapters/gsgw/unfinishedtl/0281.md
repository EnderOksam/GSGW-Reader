# Chapter 280

I looked at the student.

A teenager with bleached hair and piercings.

Inside this high school ghost story—one that could only be entered through dreams on the night of the new moon—a being that killed people when unseen and froze when observed.

And yet, if you figured out the method, it was something that could communicate with intelligence and emotion ….

*'A person.'*

High school students—citizens trapped within Segwang Special City.

My head spun.

Then that meant… every single one of those entities that died miserably every new moon, at the hands of Daydream Inc. employees and Disaster Management Bureau agents….

'.......'

I placed a hand over my uniform.

The state I was in—contaminated by Segwang Technical High School—didn’t allow overwhelming terror. Instead, I remained trapped in a strange rigidity, unable to break out of a certain frame.

Unless anger was triggered, I stayed in a fundamentally hollow state.

It was fortunate.

That this dizziness was dulled.

And that I could continue asking questions.
