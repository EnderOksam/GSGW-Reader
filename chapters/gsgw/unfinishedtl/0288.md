# Chapter 287

My mind felt blank.

Beyond the hallway-side window of the classroom stood the monster. That monster was the embodiment of miserable, skin-crawling horror, a grotesque mass of tangled human bodies. And the gaze of the upper torso lodged within the split-open center—was directed here.

The owner of that gaze.

The leader of Black Tortoise Team 1.

The Elder’s ruined upper body.

***AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHH!***

Why was she here?

As though answering that question, realization struck me.

*‘It was Segwang Special City.’*

The ghost story where the Elder lost her upper body—was here.

Among the agents who had unofficially entered Segwang Special City while on leave—was Team Leader Park Hongrim.

And the one who used her dokkaebi fire to create the talisman—

*‘It was the Team Leader.’*

Perhaps she had even “lost” that upper body intentionally.

Using the library checkout slip from Hanbit Library to hastily create the talisman in exchange for sacrificing her own torso.

*‘And the teachers lying on the fifth floor too….’*

Perhaps they had performed some kind of ritual to protect the students. Like a defensive formation.

But then something went wrong.

The paper talisman and glass lantern buried in the backyard had been damaged…

And that was what turned her into this.

#rThere you arer#

The form of contamination.

It was getting closer.

Students in school uniforms fled through the hallway only to be crushed to death. An appearance that had come too early, breaking the order of the game.

As though even the structure of a horror game could no longer contain the invasion of the impure thing.

The hallway twisted as it became covered in flesh.

The school lost its shape and was devoured.

***AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHH!***

It was coming.

It was coming, it was almost her—

*Puhk.*

I turned my head.

Inside the classroom, Lee Jaheon was shooting people.

'!'
