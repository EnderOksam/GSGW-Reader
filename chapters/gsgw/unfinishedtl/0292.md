My human form.

#><◎Egg Mood Lamp◎><#

The sort made from soft material in the shape of cute characters or objects.

But if this was the repayment, then naturally it was not an ordinary item.

An 'Item.'

A supernatural phenomenon classified as a ghost story, one that would return a human form to me.

And since it originated from the Space Shopping Mall, it was guaranteed not to be lethally dangerous.

The resulting lifeform may also be used independently after birth.

Directly use the nerves and muscles of the observed lifeform!

Like the blood bath I bought for Braun.
