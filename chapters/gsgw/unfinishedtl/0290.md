# Chapter 289

The sound of footsteps heading toward the library was fast, frequent, and quiet.

Our group—made up of two students in Segwang Technical High School uniforms and four others in various uniforms from different high schools—moved while holding our breath.

With desperate, exhausted expressions that no ordinary students should have worn.

We moved while avoiding the writhing limbs and breathing of the victims attached to the walls and floor.

Then—

“The floor.”

At Agent Bronze’s words, everyone’s gaze shifted downward.

“Look at the floor.”

"...!"

The flesh had started writhing again.

Everyone’s footsteps immediately quickened.

At least one thing was fortunate. We did not need to go upstairs.

“Here!”

At the very end of the first floor.

Following me, we stopped before the lone sliding wooden door and opened it.

The place Lee Gyeol and I had visited while returning from Hanbit Library.

[Library Room]

*Creeeak.*

With a short sound, the inside was revealed.

Like Hanbit Library, it was a place lined with bookshelves packed with books, yet its structure carried the grounded realism of an actual school library.

And then—

“This bookshelf.”

I immediately found the exact shelf.

The single bookshelf beside the window—when the hidden book was discovered, it would slide backward and become a passage leading to another section of Hanbit Library….

“…A passage connected to a supernatural phenomenon inhabited by an immensely powerful existence.”

But—

“Wait, this… doesn’t seem to open during the new moon.”

"......"

“Transfer student, the book’s gone.”

We could not open the passage hidden behind the bookshelf.

On the day the ghost story 'Within the Black Shadow' was active, the very book that allowed access to Hanbit Library simply did not exist on the shelf.

For a moment, my heart went cold, but I immediately forced myself to think straight.

*'Even so, the fact that this school exists inside Hanbit Library hasn’t changed.'*

And neither had the fact that there used to be a passage behind this bookshelf leading into Hanbit Library.

“It’s okay.”

I hurriedly pieced together a conclusion in my head as I spoke.

“If the passage isn’t appearing right now… then how about this instead? We hide the talisman exactly where the passage is supposed to appear.”

“…! Hide it directly where the passage overlaps? Behind the bookshelf?”

“Yes.”

“That’s not bad. Grapes really does come up with good ideas. Then agents, let’s put some muscle into—”

“Section Chief, I’d like you to move the bookshelf to the side.”

“Yes.”

Without any preparation whatsoever, Section Chief Lee Jaheon immediately lifted the bookshelf and set it aside.

“…Mr. Lizard, are you still not interested in changing jobs?”

“No.”

“What a waste.”

Even while speaking almost jokingly, the agents’ expressions remained serious.

Agent Bronze hurriedly looked at the wall revealed behind the moved bookshelf. And I saw it too. A metallic rectangular compartment—the exact same kind Go Yeongeun had hidden me inside back at Casino Station.

That!

“I think we can put it inside the fire hose cabinet!”

“The fire hose cabinet…?”

“Yes. Then it’d be perfect—”

But I fell silent after seeing the agents’ expressions.

No way.

“To me, it just looks like plain white wallpaper.”

"......."

“I can’t see any fire hose cabinet.”

Damn it.

Apparently this too was an element not implemented into the game, making it invisible to people who had entered 'Within the Black Shadow' as errors.

“It’s fine. Just like with the backyard, someone who can see it can do it.”

I immediately reached out my hand.

“Give it to me. I’ll—”

#rThe game’s over, so why can’t I see you?r#

We moved without even breathing.

The glass lantern was passed into my hands. I hurriedly hugged it against myself and opened the fire hose cabinet, making space inside it.

“If there’s dust or soot, wipe it clean first.”

My hands frantically searched through the inside of the cabinet, gathering dust.

Behind me, I could hear the agents moving as if guarding the outside—but I couldn’t even bring myself to turn around.

#rFoundyoutoolater#

Quickly, quickly.

“Use the cleanest part possible. Wipe everything off your hands with the inside of your sleeve. Hurry. Thoroughly.”

The whisper was so faint it was almost inaudible. Holding my breath, I brushed the dust from my hands….
