# Chapter 211 - Security Isolation Room, II

The monster's fingers, wrapped in black gloves, moved as if sweeping upward.

With that smooth movement, all the basement buttons in the elevator lit up.

The new employees stared blankly at the sight.

Now.

What did it just do?

"Ca, cancel! Ca..."

Too late.

The elevator moved a single floor in the blink of an eye.

*Ding.*

***[This is the Twelfth Basement Floor.]***

The elevator doors opened, completely unguarded.

What had happened here just hours ago flashed through like a memory.

*-- Uuuuuh...... Huuhp.*

*-- W-What is this...?*

When the elevator had gone to Basement Floor 12 to complete a 'mission', it had returned without a single employee, only a pool of red blood spread across the floor...

***[The doors are opening.]***

And now, in the glow from the elevator, the dark hallway of Basement Floor 12 dimly revealed itself. The new employees held their breath.

"......"

"......"

But there was not a single drop of blood in the hallway. Unlike inside the elevator.

"Haah."

Before they could instinctively feel relief, something odd caught their eyes. Oily dirt-like substances were piled up on the floor, stuck in the cracks of the elevator.

'Dirt...?'

Looking for the source, they lifted their heads.

They saw lumps scattered through the corridor like landmarks.

Lumps that seemed weathered with time.

But on closer inspection, eyes accustomed to the darkness could distinguish their silhouettes.

An ankle near the ankle bone, an ear and part of a temple, a right shoulder blade...

Fragments of human bodies, left colorless like mannequins.

"Hiiiee..."

*Mmpf.*

The new employee clamped a hand over his mouth. To keep any sound from escaping.

He must not make any noise.

Because, at the end of his gaze, he saw something huge.

Something standing in the dark corridor.

It, too, was wearing a uniform.

A Security Team uniform, just like the horned monster in this elevator was wearing. Neat and unaltered, a perfectly human silhouette. As if a bored Security Team member was standing there absentmindedly. But...

Its head was a scarecrow's.

A face, awkwardly drawn in fresh blood, on a roughly made, straw-stuffed, empty sack.

......

*Angry Mister Scarecrow*

A new sound.

*Cr-cr-cr-crack-crack-crack-crack-crack*

A bizarre noise echoed down the hallway.

The new employees held their breath, gasping, as they listened.

The scarecrow's form, now blacked out by smoke, wasn't even visible as a shadow.

Only the sound remained.

Only the ruthless, rending noise could be heard.

*Crunchcrunchcrunch.* It sounded like horns or bones breaking, or like something being ground together, or forcibly crammed or swallowed somewhere.

And then a wailing, as if begging, could be heard.

The sound of someone pleading and trying to escape was mixed with the tremendous noise... And soon, with a final, strangled gasp, it disappeared.

The moment silence returned.

"......"

"......"

At last, the black smoke dispersed again.

The scarecrow, which had been hidden by the smoke, reappeared in the hallway---

"...Huh?"

A corpse.

The new employees blinked.

Blood dripped from the scarecrow's makeshift features onto the ground.

*Plop.*

***[The doors are closing.]***

"O-Over there!"

The other new employees were escaping safely.

At the same time, the black smoke that had surged through the Basement Floor 12 hallway now cleanly pooled at the floor of this elevator.

"Hiieek!"

"Wait!"

As he tried desperately to avoid the smoke, someone grabbed his shoulder.

His coworker named Jang Heoun. He whispered urgently.

"Don't get on its bad side."

"...!!"

"That smoke hasn't covered our heads yet... Calm down, let's calm down."

He was right.

The smoke was quietly pooling on the elevator floor.

"Huff, huuuff..."

The new employee swallowed, catching his breath, and grabbed the elevator handrail.

***[The doors are closing.]***

And thus, they left 12BF.

He desperately looked away from the corpse that would be just beyond the elevator doors, but just before the doors closed, he thought he saw it staggering to its feet again...

Like a scarecrow.

"Huuuuh."

He swallowed with a groan.

But the intense impression of what he had just seen didn't leave him.

A bizarre being.

And the smoke that had turned that bizarre being into a corpse.

Suddenly, he realized something shocking.

'...We survived.'

Everyone had survived.

His legs shook with relief.

At the same time, another thought crossed his mind.

'Wait.'

Then maybe that strange, horned Security Team member from Room 666 was actually helping people escape via the elevator...?

'Wait, if that's true...'

Yes. Maybe there was nothing to be afraid of. Maybe it was fine! Unable to bear being trapped in an elevator with something so horrific, he tried to rationalize it, turning his head.

"Uh, excuse me..."

He made eye contact with the Security Team member.

However.

And it had thirty-seven eyes.

All the eyes stared at him, unblinking.

"Uh, uuuuuu..."

What is that?

That thing, what is it?
